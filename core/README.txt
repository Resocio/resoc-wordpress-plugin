Craft the appearance of your site when your visitors share it on Facebook

== Description ==

Would you like to know how your posts will look like when shared on Facebook by your visitors? This Facebook editor lets craft your OpenGraph metadata so *you* decide of your sharing design, not Facebook.

Will the title fit the space allocated by Facebook in its news feed? Will the description be truncated? Wonder no more, the editor shows you its final look, whatever it is on desktop, iPhone or Android smartphone.

You can even choose the format of the image Facebook will display. Again, you are in charge of the final aspect, not Facebook.

Using Yoast SEO? Perfect. We replace its classic Facebook OpenGraph editor with our.

== Installation ==

Installing "Resoc Social Editor" can be done either by searching for "Resoc Social Editor" via the "Plugins > Add New" screen in your WordPress dashboard, or by using the following steps:

1. Download the plugin via WordPress.org
1. Upload the ZIP file through the 'Plugins > Add New > Upload' screen in your WordPress dashboard
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
